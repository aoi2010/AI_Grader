## ğŸ¯ Quick Workflow Example

1. **Create Exam:**
   - Enter details
   - (Optional) Set custom duration to 60 minutes
   - Click "Generate Exam Paper"

2. **Start Exam:**
   - Read instructions
   - Click "Start Exam"

3. **Answer Questions:**
   - See question grid below the answer area
   - Click question **#5** to jump there
   - Type your answer
   - Click "Save Answer"
   - Click **#1** to go back to question 1
   - Use **Next Question** or click **#6** to jump ahead

4. **Finish:**
   - Click "Finish Exam" when ready
   - Submit your exam
